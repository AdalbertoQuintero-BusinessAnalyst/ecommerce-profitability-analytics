# Multi-Channel E-Commerce Profitability & Margin Analysis

## Project Overview
This business intelligence project investigates a multi-channel e-commerce brand that achieved strong top-line revenue ($1.4M+) but faced significant margin contraction in its direct website channel. Using Power BI and advanced DAX, I reverse-engineered the transactional data to isolate the profit leaks and deliver data-driven strategic pivots.

## Key Business Insights Uncovered
1. **The Coupon Profit Leak:** Cross-filtering customer segments and coupon distributions revealed that the direct website channel was heavily propped up by aggressive discount codes (`get4free`, `welcome2021`), which drastically eroded product value and default margins.
2. **The Operational Pivot:** While website margins suffered from heavy promotional reliance, physical Point of Sale (POS) and specific regional marketplace networks sustained highly stable, premium gross margins (73.68%). 
3. **Strategic Recommendation:** Transition marketing capital away from value-diluting digital coupons and double down on localized asset networks and marketplace channels to restore net profitability.

## Technical Skills Applied
- **Data Modeling:** Built optimized star-schema relationship paths to enable seamless cross-filtering across tables.
- **Advanced DAX:** Engineered custom context-aware measures including Gross Margin %, Average Order Value (AOV), and time-series transactional trends.
